About Me

My name is Audrey Fernandez and I have been working as a Graphic Artist in Orange County, California for over 22 years. I have an extensive background in all areas of graphic design, especially in offset and direct screen printing. Due to the wide spread shift from print to web based mediums, I have shifted my focus to web design and development utilizing HTML and CSS.

I started my career after taking classes in 1995 at the University of California, Irvine as part of the Graphic Design and Visual Communications Program. Over the years I've worked as an Art Director, Creative Director, Production Artist, Graphic Designer, and Web Designer. To remedy this, I am once again attending classes at UCI but this time as part of the Coding Boot Camp program.
